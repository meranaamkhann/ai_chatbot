Before you ask

Does it diagnose or prescribe anything?
No. It gives general, educational information and consistently points to a licensed clinician for anything specific to you, including medication dosages.
Is my conversation private?
Yes — it's tied to your account and encrypted. It's never used for training, and you can delete any conversation, or your whole account, at any time from settings.
What happens if I ask something unrelated to health?
You'll get a short, friendly redirect back to health topics instead of an answer — that's by design, not a bug.
